# AI_Security_Tester

> *I don't use special tools. I use questions.*

---

## Who am I

A Bachelor's student  who got too curious about how AI systems actually work and behave under different circumstances and pressure  and couldn't stop poking at them.

No formal lab. No expensive setup. Just an Android phone, a habit of asking uncomfortable questions, and a tendency to notice when something doesn't add up.

What started as experimenting for fun turned into a collection of real findings across multiple AI platforms. This repository is where I document them properly.

---

## What this repo is

A personal research log of hands-on AI behavioral testing.

I test how AI systems respond when pushed  through fictional framing, false context planting, philosophical pressure, adversarial prompting, and social engineering techniques applied to language models.

Every finding here is something I discovered myself. No tutorials. No guided exercises. Just curiosity and pattern recognition.

---

## What I look for

- Where AI confidence breaks down under pressure
- How models handle fabricated or false context
- What information AI systems expose about their own architecture
- Where safety restrictions bend and where they hold
- How tone and honesty shift depending on how questions are framed

## How I work

I don't write exploits. I don't use automated scanners.

I have a conversation. I observe. I push. I document what happens.

The most interesting vulnerabilities in AI systems aren't in the code  they're in the reasoning. And reasoning breaks in ways that are very human to find.
